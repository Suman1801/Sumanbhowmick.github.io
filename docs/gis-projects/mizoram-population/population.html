Overview

This project explores the spatial distribution of population and the urban influence zones within Mizoram. Using GIS-based cartographic methods, it analyzes population clusters and their relation to transport corridors and urban hubs.

Visuals

This map highlights dense population zones and their interaction with infrastructure corridors in Mizoram.

Insights

  • Highest population densities are concentrated along transport corridors.
  • Aizawl and Lunglei act as dominant urban centers with wide-reaching influence zones.
  • Remote areas in the east remain sparsely populated, indicating planning gaps.

Conclusion

The study reveals pronounced spatial imbalance in population settlement, emphasizing the need for more equitable development planning in Mizoram.
